CYBERSECURITY AWARENESS

BACKGROUND

Digital technologies has become an essential part of everyday life. Individuals and organizations rely heavily on internet for communication, education, business transactions, and entertainment. Despite these benefits many users lack adequate knowledge about cybersecurity threats and safe online behavior. Cyber criminals often exploit this lack of awareness to gain unauthorized access to personal and organizational information. Therefore, promoting cybersecurity and encouraging safety digital practices are necessary to reduce cyber risk and improved online security.

OBJECTIVES

Cybersecurity awareness plays a crucial role in todays world, it safeguard the cyber space (Environment) and also protect digital devices from cyber attacks. Moreover, it also aid individuals to understand online threats and protective measures and also helps organizations to improve its cybersecurity culture and reducing security incidents. Additionally, cybersecurity awareness create opportunity for trust and safety among customers and individuals who heavily rely on digital marketing knowing well that updating software patches and transmitting transactions information through a secured tunnel will eventually reduced attacks and threat on the cyber space.
