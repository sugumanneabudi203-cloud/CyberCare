Cybersecurity Awareness on Digital Platforms

Key Terms

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practices of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from cyber attacks, unauthorized access, and digital threats.

Digital Platform

A digital platform is an online environment such as websites, mobile apps, or social media where users communicate, share information, and perform different activities.

Cyber Threat

A cyber threat ia any malicious activity that attempts to steal, damage, or disrupt computer systems or digital information

Malware

Malware refers to harmful software such as viruses, worms,spyware, ransomware, and trojans designed to damage or gain unauthorized access to computer systems.

Password

A password is a secret combination of characters used to verify a user's identity and secure access to an account or system

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication is an additional security method that requires users to provide two form of verification before accessing an an account.

Dta Privacy

Data privacy refers tto the proper handling, storage,and protection of personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Firewall

Firewall is a security system that monitors and control incoming andd outgoing network traffic based on security rules.

Encryption

Encryption is the process of converting readable information into coded text to prevent unauthorized access.
