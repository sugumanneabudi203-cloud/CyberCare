SECURITY TIPS ON DIGITAL PLATFORMS

Here are some security tips on digital platforms:

1. Use strong, Unique password

Mix 12+ characters: A-Z, 0-9, symbols. Never reuse password. Use a password manager.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication 2FA.

Add a code from your phone/email on top of your password. Even if hackers get your password, they are stuck.

3. Keep software and Apps updated.

Updates patch security holes. Turn on auto-update for your phone, browser, and apps.

4. Beware of phishing scams.

Don't click weird links or download attachments from unknown senders. Check the email address first.

5. Use secured wi-fi + VPN.

Avoid public/free Wi-fi for banking. A VPN encrypts your data on open networks.

6. Think before you click/share.

Checks privacy settings on social media. Don't overshare personal information, location or documents
